## Premium Add-Ons (Per 30-Min Session)

### 1. Native Language Teacher Premium

Students can pay extra for a teacher who speaks their native language at a certified level. This helps when the student's English level isn't high enough for full immersion.

**Base price assumes:** Teacher is still learning your native language but not yet at B1 level.

| Language Level | Premium | Explainer |
|----------------|---------|-----------|
| Below B1 (base) | EUR 0.00 | Teacher is learning your language but not yet conversational. Can help with basic translations. |
| B1- | EUR 2.50 | Elementary. Can understand simple sentences and common phrases in your language. |
| B1 | EUR 4.00 | Intermediate. Can handle main points on familiar matters (work, school, leisure). Can explain grammar concepts in your language when needed. |
| B1+ | EUR 5.50 | Upper Intermediate. Can interact with fluency on familiar topics. Can explain complex grammar nuances and idiomatic expressions in your language. |
| B2 | EUR 7.00 | Advanced. Can understand complex texts and technical discussions. Can explain subtleties of English grammar by comparing structures in your language. |

**Explainer popup content (for the ? icon next to each level):**

> **Below B1 (included):** Your teacher is still learning your native language. They know basic words and phrases but cannot yet hold a conversation in it. They can help with simple vocabulary lookups.
>
> **B1- (EUR 2.50/session):** Your teacher can understand the main points of clear, familiar language. They know common everyday expressions. For example, they can help you understand the difference between "make" and "do" by comparing to how your language handles these concepts.
>
> **B1 (EUR 4.00/session):** Your teacher can deal with most situations likely to arise in your language. They can describe experiences, events, and ambitions. For instance, they can explain why English uses "the" in some cases but not others by comparing to your language's article system.
>
> **B1+ (EUR 5.50/session):** Your teacher can interact with a degree of fluency and spontaneity. They can explain nuanced grammar points. For example, they can clarify the difference between present perfect and past simple by showing how your language expresses completed actions differently from English.
>
> **B2 (EUR 7.00/session):** Your teacher has a high level of proficiency. They can understand demanding texts and use language flexibly. They can explain subtle differences like "say" vs "tell" vs "speak" vs "talk" by mapping them to distinct verbs in your language.

## Pricing Calculator UI Specification

The pricing page must include an interactive calculator where prospective clients can:

1. **Select course type** (dropdown): Young Learners, Conversational Adult, Exam Prep, Business English
2. **Select bundle size** (toggle): Pay-as-you-go, 12, 24, 48, 64
3. **Select teacher language level** (dropdown with ? info popup): Below B1, B1-, B1, B1+, B2
4. **Toggle industry experience** (checkbox + industry multi-select): +EUR 6.00
5. **Toggle accent coaching** (checkbox + track selector): +EUR 5.00
6. **See real-time price update:** Per-session price (large), monthly estimate (small), total bundle price (medium)

**Display format:**
```
EUR 26.50 /session          ← LARGEST (Space Grotesk, 2.4rem, bold)
EUR 212.00/mo (8 sess)      ← MEDIUM (Inter, 0.9rem)
Bundle: EUR 636.00 (24 sess) ← SMALL (Inter, 0.75rem, muted)
```
