## 1. Overview

Articulate English is the public-facing brand of the Eloquenta teaching network. We connect qualified English teachers with individual learners worldwide. This document explains how teachers are paid, what they earn, and how to advance to our corporate brand (Eloquenta) for higher rates.

Our goal is to be one of the best-paying platforms in online ESL. We believe great teachers deserve great pay, and that investing in teachers is the best way to deliver outstanding lessons.

## 2. Teacher Tiers and Pay Rates

All rates are per 30-minute session.

### Tier 1 — New Teacher (Articulate English)

**Requirements:**
- Recognized English teaching certification (TESOL, TEFL, CELTA, or equivalent)
- Native or C2-level English proficiency
- Stable internet connection (minimum 10 Mbps upload)
- Working webcam and microphone
- Pass a demo lesson evaluation

**Base Pay:** EUR 7.00 per 30-min session

| Milestone | Rate |
|-----------|------|
| First 100 lessons | EUR 7.00 |
| 100+ lessons, 4.7+ rating | EUR 8.00 |
| 500+ lessons, 100+ reviews | EUR 9.00 |

### Tier 2 — Experienced Teacher (Eloquenta Corporate)

**Requirements:**
- 500+ completed lessons on Articulate English
- Minimum 100 reviews with 4.7+ average rating
- B1 or higher in a second language (any language)
- Completed Eloquenta teacher training and exam
- Verified stable tech setup (internet backup, power backup, computer backup)
- Passed mock corporate lesson evaluation

**Base Pay:** EUR 11.00–14.00 per 30-min session

| Factor | Rate |
|--------|------|
| Base corporate rate | EUR 11.00 |
| 4.8+ corporate client rating | EUR 12.50 |
| Industry specialization | EUR 14.00 |

## 3. Premium Add-On Revenue Sharing

When students pay for premium add-ons, the revenue is shared 50/50 with the teacher to incentivize skill development.

### Teacher Language Level Premium

If you certify in a student's native language, you earn 50% of the language premium:

| Your Language Level | Student Pays | You Earn (50% of premium) |
|--------------------|-------------|--------------------------|
| B1- | +EUR 2.50/session | +EUR 1.25/session |
| B1 | +EUR 4.00/session | +EUR 2.00/session |
| B1+ | +EUR 5.50/session | +EUR 2.75/session |
| B2 | +EUR 7.00/session | +EUR 3.50/session |

**Example:** A B1+ teacher teaching exam prep for a student who selected B1+ language support earns: EUR 8.00 (base) + EUR 2.75 (language premium share) = EUR 10.75/session.

### Industry Experience Premium

If you have verified industry experience, you earn 50% of the industry premium:

- Student pays: +EUR 6.00/session
- You earn: +EUR 3.00/session

### Accent Coaching Premium

If you are certified for accent coaching, you earn 50% of the accent premium:

- Student pays: +EUR 5.00/session
- You earn: +EUR 2.50/session

### Full Premium Example

A teacher with B1+ in German, finance industry experience, and accent coaching certification, teaching exam prep to a German-speaking student who selects all three premiums:

- Base pay: EUR 8.00
- Language premium share (B1+): +EUR 2.75
- Industry premium share: +EUR 3.00
- Accent premium share: +EUR 2.50
- **Total: EUR 16.25/session**

## 7. Cancellation Policy

| Scenario | Teacher Compensation |
|----------|---------------------|
| Student cancels 24+ hours before | No pay (slot was available) |
| Student cancels less than 24 hours before | 50% of session rate |
| Student no-show | 100% of session rate |
| Teacher cancels for emergency (with notice) | No penalty, first 2 per month |
| Platform technical failure | 100% of session rate |
