Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free trial work?▼
Book a free 30-minute session with one of our teachers. We will assess your level, discuss your goals, and show you how the platform works. No payment required.
What is the AI translation widget?▼
When a concept cannot be explained in English alone, AI translates key terms into your home language in real-time during the lesson. This is included in every session at no extra cost.
How do I choose a teacher who speaks my language?▼
Use the teacher directory to filter by language level. Choose from Below B1 (learning) to B2 (proficient). Each level has an explanation popup with examples of what the teacher can explain in your language.
What does B1 or B2 mean for teacher language levels?▼
These are CEFR levels. B1 means the teacher can handle main points on familiar matters and explain grammar concepts in your language. B2 means they can explain subtle differences and technical vocabulary. See the full breakdown on the pricing page.
How do bundles work?▼
Buy 12, 24, 48, or 64 sessions upfront and save 10-25%. Sessions never expire. Use them at your own pace. The discount applies to the base rate — premiums are added at full price.
Can I cancel or reschedule?▼
Cancel or reschedule up to 24 hours before your session for a full refund. Late cancellations (less than 24 hours) receive 50%. No-shows are charged the full rate.
What exams do you prepare for?▼
IELTS, TOEFL, Cambridge (B2 First, C1 Advanced), OET, PTE, Duolingo English Test, CELPIP, TOEIC, and more. All major English exams are covered.
How is Articulate English different from Eloquenta?▼
Speak Easy English is for individual learners. Eloquenta is our corporate B2B brand for companies. Teachers start here and can graduate to Eloquenta for higher pay as they gain experience.

How Does the Free Trial Work?

This free trial is your chance to book one session with me so we can talk through your own or your family's learning, practicing, editing or translation goals.

Choose either a full 30-minute session, or a quick 15-minute mini-session if you are pressed for time. Both are completely free and carry no obligation.

The aim is a friendly, relaxed lesson environment — think of it as a casual conversation, not a test. If any word feels unclear, I will translate it for you in the chat. I will also be typing corrections live during the session, so we can already start addressing any knowledge gaps in the trial homework.

You will get a quick tour of the platform and a demonstration of some interactive features and learning activities, so you know exactly what to expect from our sessions.

For example, B1 is divided into four levels (B1.0, B1.1, B1.2, B1.3), showing precisely where you are and what to tackle next., receive a custom course design outline with prep homework for your first class, and learn how to book future sessions from the smartphone app or website.

There is no need to feel anxious about the assessment. It will feel like a casual chat, and you will leave with a clear plan.
