Pricing FAQ

Are there any hidden fees?▼
No. The price you see is the price you pay. AI translation is included in every session at no extra cost. Premiums (language level, industry, accent) are clearly shown before you book.
Do sessions expire?▼
No. Once you buy a bundle, your sessions never expire. Use them at your own pace — whether that is 4 sessions a week or 2 per month.
Can I change my teacher later?▼
Yes. You can switch teachers at any time at no cost. Your sessions transfer with you. We recommend trying 2-3 teachers to find the best fit.
What payment methods do you accept?▼
We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfer. Payment is secure and processed through Stripe.
How does the language level premium work?▼
The base price includes a teacher who is learning your language. If you want a teacher certified at B1 or above in your native language, you pay a premium per session. This lets them explain grammar by comparing structures between English and your language.
Can I get a refund?▼
Cancel 24+ hours before a session for a full refund. Late cancellations (less than 24 hours) receive 50%. No-shows are charged the full rate. Unused bundle sessions can be refunded within 30 days of purchase.
