FAQ

What level do I start at?▼
We assess your level during the free trial. Most adults start between FL3-FL7. Young learners start at FL0-FL2.
Can I switch between young learners and adult lessons?▼
Yes. The platform adapts to the student. Parents can manage their child's account separately.
Is AI translation really free?▼
Yes. AI translation into your home language is included in every session at no extra cost.
How young can my child be?▼
We accept learners from age 6. Our young learner teachers are specially trained for children and teens.
