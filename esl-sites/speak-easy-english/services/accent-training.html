FAQ

Which track is right for me?▼
Choose Accent Understanding if you struggle to understand different English speakers. Choose Accent Reduction if you want to modify your own accent for professional clarity.
How long does it take?▼
Most students notice improvement within 8-12 sessions. Significant change typically takes 24-48 sessions of consistent practice.
Can I do both tracks?▼
Yes. Many students start with understanding and then move to reduction. We can combine both in a single program.
Do I need any special equipment?▼
Just a working microphone and headphones. Everything else is handled through our platform.
