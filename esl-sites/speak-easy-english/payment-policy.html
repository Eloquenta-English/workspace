Teacher Payment Policy

Effective: [Launch Date] | Version 1.1 | Public version for prospective teachers

Overview

Articulate English is the public-facing brand of the Eloquenta teaching network. We connect qualified English teachers with individual learners worldwide. Our goal is to be one of the best-paying platforms in online ESL.

Teacher Tiers

Teachers progress through tiers based on experience: New Teacher (EUR 7/session), Established (EUR 8/session after 100+ lessons at 4.7+ rating), and Experienced (EUR 9/session after 500+ lessons). Corporate teachers at Eloquenta earn EUR 11-14/session.

Premium Revenue Sharing

When students select premium add-ons, the revenue is shared 50/50 with the teacher. This includes teacher language level premiums (EUR 2.50-7.00/session), industry experience (EUR 6.00/session), and accent coaching (EUR 5.00/session).

Cancellation Policy

Full pay if student no-shows. 50% pay if student cancels less than 24 hours before. No pay if student cancels 24+ hours before (slot was available). Full pay for platform technical failures.

Payment Schedule

Weekly payouts for the first 3 months to build trust. Bi-weekly payouts after that. All payouts via Wise or Payoneer with fees absorbed by the platform.

What We Provide

Free training and certification. Lesson tools including AI translation. Scheduling autonomy. Transparent earnings breakdown with every payout. Clear career path to Eloquenta corporate teaching.

Path to Eloquenta

After 500+ lessons, 100+ reviews at 4.7+ rating, B1+ in a second language, and completing corporate training, teachers can graduate to our Eloquenta corporate brand for significantly higher pay (EUR 11-14/session).

How to Apply

Visit our Become a Teacher page to submit an application. Requirements include a recognized teaching certification, stable internet, webcam, and passing a demo lesson evaluation.
