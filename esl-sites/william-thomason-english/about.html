About William

William Thomason has spent years teaching corporate English to professionals at major brands — from engineers and developers to executives and their families. He knows exactly how to tailor-make efficient packages that have you feeling more confident about success from the trial lesson onward.

"I built this site myself because I was tired of the shortcomings of large platforms — their slow progress, one-course-for-everyone mentality, bright pages that are harsh on the eyes, and teachers who struggle to design effective courses."

William is fluent in German — not from a classroom, but from years of living and working there while travelling across Europe. It was in Germany that he first started teaching English, cutting his teeth on real conversations with real people who needed the language for their careers. "Living in Germany gave me the foundation. I learned how it feels to be the one struggling with a new language — and that shapes everything I do as a teacher."

He also spent a great deal of time in Spain, and has B1 Business Spanish. "You do not need to be perfect. You need to be understood. That is the mindset I bring to every lesson."

William is passionate about IT and loves making content and apps. He developed this platform from scratch to address the real problems professionals face: generic curricula, eye-straining interfaces, and a lack of teachers who understand technical workplaces.

He is currently sharpening his own Spanish skills so he can better serve his students. "If I am going to push my learners to keep growing, I had better be doing the same myself."

Every feature on this site — from the dark-mode-first design to the AI-powered translation — was built with one goal: get you communicating confidently in the shortest time possible.
