# Eloquenta — Sitemap (Corporate B2B)

**Brand:** Eloquenta (corporate B2B)
**Positioning:** Professional English training for companies and organizations
**Target:** HR departments, L&D teams, corporate training managers
**Teacher pay:** EUR 22–28/hr

## Page Descriptions

### index.html — Landing Page
**Purpose:** Convert corporate decision-makers to booking a demo
**Sections:**
1. Hero — "Professional English training for your team" + CTA (Book a demo / Get a quote)
2. Problem statement — Why English skills matter for business
3. Solutions overview — 4 cards (Business English, Exam Prep, Accent Training, Onboarding)
4. How it works — 3 steps (Assess needs → Match teachers → Track progress)
5. Social proof — Client logos + testimonials
6. Pricing preview — Starting from EUR 60/hr, link to full pricing
7. FAQ — 6-8 corporate-focused questions
8. CTA — "Book a free consultation"
9. Footer — Links, social, legal

### /solutions/ — Solution Pages
**Purpose:** Explain each corporate offering in detail
**Each solution page includes:**
- Business case (ROI, impact on team)
- Program structure
- Teacher qualifications
- Customization options
- Pricing
- CTA to book a consultation

### /pricing/ — Corporate Pricing Page
**Purpose:** Transparent corporate pricing
**Content:**
- Per-session rate by service type
- Package discounts
- Group training pricing (teams of 2-10)
- Enterprise pricing (custom quote)
- What's included (progress reports, account manager, etc.)

### /contact/ — Contact Page
**Purpose:** Convert to qualified lead
**Features:**
- Contact form (name, company, email, team size, interest)
- Calendar widget to book a 30-minute consultation
- Phone number (if applicable)
- Office location

### /client/dashboard.html — Client Admin Dashboard
**Purpose:** Give HR/L&D teams visibility and control
**Content:**
- Team enrollment overview
- Upcoming lesson schedule
- Employee progress tracking
- Teacher ratings and feedback
- Billing and invoices
- Downloadable reports

## Key Differences from Articulate English

| Aspect | Eloquenta | Articulate English |
|--------|----------|-------------------|
| Audience | Corporate B2B | Individual consumers |
| Pricing display | Per-session + monthly total | Per-session rate prominent |
| Teacher direction | Assigned by account manager | Student chooses |
| Booking | Account manager arranged | Self-service |
| Reporting | Detailed progress for HR | Simple progress for student |
| Teacher pay | EUR 22-28/hr | EUR 12-18/hr |
| Brand feel | Professional, corporate | Warm, accessible |
| Color scheme | Cool dark (cyan + blue) | Warm dark (mustard + burgundy) |
| Tone | Formal, results-oriented | Friendly, encouraging |
