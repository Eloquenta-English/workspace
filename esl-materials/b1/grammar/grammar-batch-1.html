Conditionals (1st, 2nd, 3rd)

If + present/past/past perfect, will/would/would have + base/pp
Different conditions for different times

Press → or Space to start

Examples - Real future

Real future

Examples - Unreal present

Unreal present

Examples - Unreal past

Unreal past

Common Mistakes

❌ "If it will rain, I will stay home."

✅ "If it rains, I will stay home."

💡 Never use 'will' in the if-clause. Use present simple after 'if'.

❌ "If I would have more time, I would travel."

✅ "If I had more time, I would travel."

💡 2nd conditional uses past simple, not 'would have'.

❌ "If you will practice, you improve."

✅ "If you practice, you will improve."

💡 Main clause needs 'will' to show future result.

Exercise

  • will go / will have
  • goes / will have
  • goes / has
  • will go / has
✅ goes / will have - Correct! Present simple in IF-clause, will + base in main clause.
