IELTS Practice

Travel & Tourism

Part 1 - Interview

Travel

Where was the last place you visited on holiday?

Think for 10 seconds, then answer in 3 sentences with reasons.

Model Answer (3 sentences with reasons)
I love travelling because it broadens my perspective and lets me experience different cultures. I prefer destinations with rich history and good food.

Would you like to go back there again?

Think for 10 seconds, then answer in 3 sentences with reasons.

Model Answer (3 sentences with reasons)
Yes, I'd definitely go back. The place left a lasting impression on me, and there are still many things I didn't have time to explore. I'm already planning my next visit.

What kind of tourist destinations do you usually prefer?

Think for 10 seconds, then answer in 3 sentences with reasons.

Model Answer (3 sentences with reasons)
I usually prefer cultural destinations over beach holidays. I love exploring historical cities, trying local cuisine, and learning about different traditions and ways of life.
Part 2 - Long Turn

Cue Card

Describe a journey you have been on.
  • Where you went
  • What kind of transport you used
  • How long the journey took
  • And say whether it was easy or difficult
1 min prepare2 min speak
Model Answer (2 minutes)
I took a train from Vienna to Budapest last year. It was a comfortable journey of about 2.5 hours through beautiful countryside. I went with two friends and we spent the weekend exploring the city.
Part 3 - Discussion

Abstract Questions

How do most people travel long distances in your country?

Give a balanced answer with examples.

Model Answer
In Austria, most people travel by train for long distances. The rail network is extensive and reliable. Budget airlines have also become very popular.

Have the types of transport changed over the last few decades?

Give a balanced answer with examples.

Model Answer
Trains have become faster and more comfortable. Budget airlines have made international travel more affordable. Electric cars are also becoming more popular.

Is it better to travel in your own country or abroad?

Give a balanced answer with examples.

Model Answer
I think both have their merits. Travelling in your own country helps you appreciate what you have, while going abroad exposes you to new ideas.
